Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Lattitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the souther hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the of the earth at the time of year this data was gathered.
